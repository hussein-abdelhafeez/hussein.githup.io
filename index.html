About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Hussein Abd Elhafeez, a dedicated Power BI Developer and Data Analyst with a strong background and a passion for transforming raw data and leveraging technology into insightful visualizations to derive valuable insights.

I graduated from Faculty of Science - Geophysics Department in 2014.

Over the past few years, I have honed my skills through various roles, where I've blended my knowledge and working experience.

I have served in many roles such as a Medical Supplies Contract Analyst at Pro Med from September 2021 to June 2023. In this role, I was responsible for procurement and supplier analysis, sourcing, and procurement, supplier management, cost efficiency, and inventory management.

Before that, I held the position of Senior Medical Purchasing at Al Assima from February 2020 to August 2021. Here, I focused on contracts and purchasing analysis, strategic procurement planning, risk management, and vendor evaluation.

Additionally, I have a solid educational foundation, having pursued a Power BI Developer Track at the Information Technology Institute and completed various courses, including the Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst Professional Certificate and the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate.

Also ShAI Club for artificial intelligence Course in Jordan Which designed specifically for junior Data scientist

My technical skills include proficiency in SQL, Python, Tableau, and Microsoft Power BI, complemented by a strong understanding of statistical concepts and machine learning algorithms, Such as XGBoost, Random Forest, KNN, and SGD Classifier.

I am excited about the prospect of contributing my skills and experience to dynamic and collaborative environments, bringing value through data-driven solutions.

Thank you for considering my profile, and I look forward to the opportunity to discuss how my background aligns with your organization's needs.
